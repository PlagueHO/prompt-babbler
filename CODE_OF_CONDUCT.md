## Our Pledge

We are committed to making participation in this community a respectful and harassment-free experience for everyone.

## Our Standards

Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment:

* Showing empathy and respect toward other people
* Giving and accepting constructive feedback
* Taking accountability and learning from mistakes
* Focusing on what is best for the community

Examples of unacceptable behavior:

* Harassment, intimidation, discrimination, or personal attacks
* Trolling, insulting comments, or inflammatory language
* Publishing private information without explicit permission
* Any conduct that is inappropriate in a professional setting

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies in all project spaces, including issues, pull requests, discussions, and other communication channels related to this repository.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers are responsible for clarifying and enforcing this Code of Conduct and may take appropriate corrective action in response to unacceptable behavior.

## Reporting

Report Code of Conduct concerns privately to project maintainers.

All reports will be reviewed and investigated promptly and fairly.
