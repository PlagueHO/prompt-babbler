## Key Learnings Documented

1. AVM managed environment uses `infrastructureSubnetId`, not `infrastructureSubnetResourceId`
1. Container Apps Consumption environments do NOT require subnet delegation
1. Minimum /23 subnet required for ACA Consumption
1. Private endpoint network policies must be explicitly disabled
1. Foundry requires both `cognitiveservices` and `openai` DNS zones
1. AVM VNET module outputs subnets in declaration order: [0] = ACA, [1] = PE

## Impact

- **Dev:** No impact (hybrid mode default)
- **Prod:** Can restrict to private-only via `enablePublicNetworkAccess=false`
- **Cost:** ~$14.60/month additional for private endpoints + VNET (free)
